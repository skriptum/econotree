You are going to play an auction game.
You will be assigned a role of either a seller or a buyer.

  • You will see at what prices the other players sold/bought their items.

You will play 5 Rounds of Trading. Each round you can sell/buy one item

Each Round takes two Minutes

If you are a Seller, you have one item to sell.

  • Your Upper Price Limit is {{C.VALUATION}}
